All About Elephants

Introduction

Elephants are the largest land mammals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence and strong social bonds within their herds.

Characteristics of Elephants

Elephants have unique features that set them apart from other animals:

  • Trunk
    • Used for feeding, drinking, and social interaction.
  • Tusks
    • Modified incisors used for digging and foraging.
  • Large Ears
    • Help regulate body temperature.

Interesting Facts

  1. Elephants can communicate using low-frequency sounds.
    1. This allows them to communicate over long distances.
  2. They have excellent memories.
    1. They can recognize individuals even after years of separation.
